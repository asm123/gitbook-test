# Better than being smart

From Ritendra Dutta's [post on Linkedin](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/ritendradatta_at-the-3-month-mark-at-openai-reflecting-activity-7451786694711750657-HikL/):

* Hunger and hustle to drive real impact
* Ability to unblock oneself when stuck
* Persistence in solving hard problems; sticking with them, but also knowing when to stop
* Occasional bursts of creativity
* Using new tools with humility and curiosity, discarding dated assumptions
* Helping coworkers with generosity and kindness
* Consistently showing up for the team
* Being boringly predictable with project-level accountability
* Not being too focused on personal growth at the expense of values and team impact
* Staying physically and mentally healthy



From Shivani Poddar's [post on LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/shivani-poddar_what-i-look-for-as-i-am-hiring-my-founding-activity-7451734079793709056-_HDM):

1. You have excelled in something in your life - could be athletics, competitive programming, anything - this shows me you have it in you to learn and be the bestest and greatest at something
2. You have founder energy - that fire in the belly and never say never attitude. I tell my team, I will be the first check in some of their companies someday.
3. AI pilled - you know how to exponentiate yourself, your stack, your product and people around you with AI
