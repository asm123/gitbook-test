# Building real skills during the AI boom

[Building Real Skills During the AI Boom - No, Not That Kind of Skill](https://overcast.fm/+JDyvztCd4) - Developer Tea (published April 22, 2026)

Takeaways:

* Do not fall into the trap of coding being the only value of a software engineer. Much of it can be automated now in the age of AI.
* Real value comes from domain expertise, systems thinking, risk and bottleneck analysis, organizational design, tech-lead-level sequencing of wok, relational skills that unblock hard moments in a company's life.
* Focus on building valuable, durable and transferable skills.
